Danger Zone (EECS 494)

Unity | C#

For this game, we were tasked with creating a rapid prototype of a game by getting the core elements done, then playtesting and iterating on the design.

Danger Zone is a puzzle game focused on the "Danger Zones": Areas that when the player enters, a enemy appears and chases them. Throughout the game the player will learn to find paths through each level, and hopefully complete the game.

This was a completely solo project.

Check it out! (itch.io)

Zelda Game Recreation (EECS 494)

Unity | C#

In this project, as a group of two we recreated the first dungeon of the NES version of the Legend of Zelda. There is also a custom mechanic and level available if you press 4, which turn the game into a sort of puzzle game.

I mainly worked on the health management and the enemies, and the level design of our custom level.

Check it out! (.zip download)
